About Me

Turning Data into Decisions

I'm a Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo with a growing focus on data analytics, machine learning, and sports analytics. I enjoy building tools that transform raw data into clear, actionable insight.

My work spans business intelligence (Power BI, SQL, DAX) and data science (Python, R, Shiny), with a particular interest in applying these skills to sports performance and strategy. I've contributed to research in NFL tracking data analytics and built real-time visualization tools for coaching staff.

Outside of data work, sport is a big part of how I think — I'm passionate about basketball, volleyball, and football (soccer), which naturally informs my analytical lens.
